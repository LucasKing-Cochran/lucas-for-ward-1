About the candidate

I'm Lucas.

I'm running for City Council because Ward 1 deserves strong progressive policy

Why I'm running

I'm a student and service worker. I experience Ann Arbor's cost-of-living crisis every single day — not in a policy briefing, but when I pay rent, go to the grocery store, or have conversations with my neighbors who are being pushed out of this city.

I'm running as an independent democratic socialist because I believe working-class representation matters when it comes to the issues our city faces. For too long, City Hall has listened to corporate donors and real estate developers while students, service workers, renters, and longtime residents go unheard.

The people of Ward 1 deserve to hear their voices heard. The issues facing us are complex and will not be solved without strong progressive voices for the working-class.

As your City Council member, I pledge to fight for public housing, tenant and labor rights, and to protect our city's future from corporate greed, federal fascism, and climate change.

My campaign is different — and that's the point.

Help me get money and partisanship out of our city politics and fight for the Ann Arbor our Ward deserves.
